Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your Rhydle account and all associated data.

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your Rhydle account, you can do so directly from within the app. The process is simple and permanent — once completed, all your data is erased from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open your profile

Tap on your user profile icon inside the app.

2

Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll down and tap the Delete Account button.

3

Provide a reason

Select or enter the reason why you are deleting your account. This helps us improve Rhydle for others.

4

Confirm deletion

Confirm your decision. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently erase all data we have for you. This includes:

  • Your profile information (name, email, avatar)
  • All business and income stream data
  • Transaction history and profit records
  • App preferences and settings
  • Any other information stored on your behalf

This action is permanent

Once your account is deleted, there is no way to recover your data. All information is completely and irreversibly removed from our systems. Only the information we need for you is deleted — we do not retain any of your personal or business data after deletion.

Need Help?

If you are having trouble deleting your account or have questions, contact us at support@rhydle.com and we will assist you.
