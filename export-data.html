Data & Privacy

Export Your Data

Your data is yours. Take it with you anytime — no account deletion required.

Your numbers should never be locked in

We built Rhydle to give you clarity on your profit — not to hold your data hostage. Every transaction you log, every business you track, every number you enter belongs to you. Not us.

That is why we give you a full data export at any time, for any reason, without needing to close your account. Want a backup? Export it. Moving to a spreadsheet? Export it. Just want peace of mind? Export it. Your information is never stuck inside the app.

How to Export Your Data

1

Open Settings

Tap the Settings icon from the app menu.

2

Go to Data & Privacy

Navigate to the Data & Privacy section.

3

Tap "Export My Data"

Hit the export button. We will package everything up for you.

4

Download your file

Your data is exported as a file you can save, share, or open in any spreadsheet app.

What You Get

Your export includes everything we store for your account:

  • All your business and income stream data
  • Complete transaction and profit history
  • Expense and revenue breakdowns
  • Your profile information
  • App settings and preferences

Export without losing anything

Exporting your data does not delete your account or remove anything from Rhydle. Your account stays active and your data stays in the app — you simply get a copy of it. Export as many times as you like.

Our Promise

No lock-in

Your data is never trapped. Export it whenever you want.

Unlimited exports

There is no limit. Export daily, weekly, or once — it is up to you.

Standard formats

Files work in Excel, Google Sheets, or any tool you already use.

Need Help?

If you have trouble exporting your data or have questions about what is included, contact us at support@rhydle.com.
