Expectations

Let's be honest.

Before you sign up, you should know exactly what you're getting — and what you're not.

Rhydle is

  • A daily profit tracker. You log what you earned and what you spent. It gives you the one number that matters.

  • Manual by design. You type in your own numbers. That's not a shortcut we skipped — it's the whole point. You see what you enter.

  • Built for multiple businesses. Each income stream gets its own project. You see them separately and together.

  • Portable. Export everything to Excel whenever you want. Your data is never locked in.

  • Simple on purpose. No 50-tab dashboards. No learning curve. Open it, log your day, close it. Done.

Rhydle isn't

  • Not an accounting tool. We don't do invoicing, tax filing, or payroll. Use Rhydle alongside your accountant, not instead of one.

  • Not automatic. We don't sync your bank or pull transactions. If you don't log it, it doesn't exist. That's the discipline that makes it work.

  • Not magic. Rhydle won't fix your business. But it will show you exactly where the money goes — what you do with that is up to you.

  • Not useful if you skip days. The data is only as good as your consistency. Miss a week and the picture is incomplete. Show up daily and it's powerful.

  • Not trying to trap you. We never ask for bank credentials. Export your data anytime. If Rhydle stops being useful, leave — no hard feelings.
